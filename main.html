Summary

Inception is a science-fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious during dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform "inception"— planting an idea into someone's mind.
